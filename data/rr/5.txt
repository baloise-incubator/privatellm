5. The Evolution of Social Media:
Social media has transformed the way we connect and communicate. From the early days of platforms like MySpace to the global dominance of Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, social media has evolved rapidly. It allows people to share their lives, opinions, and experiences with a vast audience. However, it also raises concerns about privacy, online bullying, and misinformation. The influence of social media on politics, activism, and marketing is undeniable. Its continued evolution will shape how we interact in the digital age.
